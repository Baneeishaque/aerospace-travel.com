About Us

  • Aerospace Travel is an emerging travel services provider company in UAE. Company was established in 2012. Aerospace Travel is operating from Abu Dhabi.
  • Our goal is to provide best possible services to suit corporate, leisure as well as individual traveler. We at Aerospace Travel provide best match of good service and competitive price.
  • Aerospace Travel is more than a travel agency. We are a business partner, a friend, a community resource. Our success comes from the success of our customers, their businesses.
  • The core values of Aerospace Travel are communication, motivation, satisfaction and partnership. Whether an individual or a seasoned business traveler, we offer the same level of service.
  • As your travel partner, we take care of our business so you have an easier time taking care of yours.
  • We are also a member of ATTAC, the governing body for travel agents in Abu Dhabi. Aerospace travel is managed by a team of highly qualified professionals who provide you personalized and round the clock service. We are also supported by a fully computerized reservation system linked directly to airlines, hotel chains etc.
